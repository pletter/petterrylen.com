I'm a pianist, composer, youtuber, beatboxer and person.

I grew up by a lake in eastern Sweden. The piano got its hooks into me when I was 14, and that's when I started playing jazz. Ever since then, all I've has been doing is playing the piano, writing piano music, improvising on the piano, teaching the piano, playing the piano, making videos about piano, thinking about piano, piano piano, piano piano piano piano.

Now I live in Newport, Wales, and I started a new chapter of my life, with one mission: writing piano music.
