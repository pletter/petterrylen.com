Through teaching these people I’ve come to experience time and time again, that learning the super power of using the piano to explore new chords and melodies and recording their own tracks, is achievable in a few months with a few minutes of daily practiced.
I’ve created a simple practice program, that step by step takes you through the few things you need to learn to feel free and confident to use the piano in your work as a producer.
